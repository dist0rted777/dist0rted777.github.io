About

Engineer by training, creative by instinct.

I'm Chidiebere Udegbe — a mechatronics engineer who likes working where hardware, software and intelligence meet. I've taken eight projects across robotics, IoT and power systems from mechanical CAD and electrical schematics to working builds — including my final-year Wi-Fi teach-and-repeat mobile robot, an automatic solar-powered maize sheller, and a hospital supply-delivery robot.

Away from the bench I'm DIST0RTED — a music producer and video editor with 20+ tracks and a run of event, advert and music-video work. The same discipline that debugs a control loop shapes a clean edit or a tight mix.

Based globally and open to remote work across engineering, AI, audio/video and game development.
